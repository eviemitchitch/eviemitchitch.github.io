Newark Wilder T-Shirt Design, 2016
A T-Shirt design made in collaboration with a friend, for his band Newark Wilder. He drew an initial design on paper, and I made adjustments to it, recreated it digitally, and formatted it for printing. My first pass prototype design is shown printed, left, and the finalized design is shown right. The majority of the design was done in Illustrator, with hand-lettering in Photoshop for a personal touch. A limited run of 25 shirts was sold at the Vanessa Atalanta album release show at the Drunken Unicorn, as well as several other concerts throughout Atlanta, with additional sales online over Bandcamp.

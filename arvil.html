Praecipitium, 2017-2018
A digital painting inspired by the works of Ferdinand Hodler and Alexandra Levasseur. As a requirement for Visual Arts in the International Baccalaureate Diploma Program, I selected three works by two different artists and studied them extensively, comparing and contrasting the works, their artists, and the influences behind them. Then, I created my own piece, Praecipitium, inspired by their works and utilizing techniques in composition, style, and content from both artists.
